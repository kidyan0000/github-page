FoldMatch

FoldMatch is a new physics-based conformal registration method, which is based on continuum mechanics and is able to match complex folding and pleating patterns of clothes.


Publications

[1] S. A. Ali, S. Yan, W. Dornisch and D. Stricker, “FoldMatch Accurate and High Fidelity Garment Fitting onto 3D Scans”, ICIP, 2716-2720, 2020
