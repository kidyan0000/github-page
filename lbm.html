Lattice Boltzmann Method

The Lattice Boltzmann Method can be interpreted as an alternative numerical approach for solving partial differential equations. The main idea behind the Lattice Boltzmann Method is to bridge the gap between the microscale and the macroscale. To achieve this, the Lattice Boltzmann Equation is separated into two steps: collision and streaming. In this work, the existing Lattice Boltzmann Method for the two-dimensional wave equation is extended to more general plane strain problems.
